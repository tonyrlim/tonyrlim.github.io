Spain as a Mission Field

Quiet Minority



Less than 1% identify as Protestant Christians. Although Spain is usually thought to be predominantly Catholic, only 18% of its citizens are actually practicing.

Smaller Churches



The average church has a congregation of under 100 people; a church of 400 people is considered very big; over 1000 is rare; the largest church is 1500 at most.

Overlooked Ministries



Local churches specifically lack logistical, practical and doctrinal aspects of Worship and Tech ministries. Worship and Tech ministries have mostly been overlooked by previous and current missionaries.
