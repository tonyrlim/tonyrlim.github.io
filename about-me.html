Who am I?


Hello and thank you for visiting! My name is Jason Hibdon, and I am embarking on a mission that is incredibly close to my heart.

Born into a musical family, I have always been drawn to the power of rhythm and a good beat. In fact, my mom's side of the family all sang and played some type of instrument. I am certainly no exception. From the early age of 13, I began to play piano in worship for my family's church.

During my late formative and early adolescent years, I had the privilege of growing up in Spain as a Missionary kid. Living amidst the beauty and challenges of a different culture shaped my perspective in profound ways. My parents, missionaries themselves, showed me firsthand the highs and the lows of the mission field.

Now, as I've grown and discerned my path, I feel called by God to return to the people and places that have held my heart since childhood. This mission isn't just a trip—it's a homecoming, a chance to give back to the communities that shaped me and continue to inspire my journey.

How my Experience supports God's Mission


While studying and working at Christ for the Nations, I produced, engineered, arranged, sang and played on numerous recordings. I also trained, equipped and mentored over a hundred students in worship ministry. As Music Director & Producer for Marco Barrientos, a renowned global Spanish Christian artist, I toured various countries, wrote songs, and produced several recordings. Under the mentorship of Randy Adams, a highly accredited recording engineer, I was part of recording over 25 live worship albums throughout the US and abroad. I signed a record deal, toured and recorded with my own band, SuperGod
